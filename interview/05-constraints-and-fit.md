## T8 checklist

Ask me to confirm or correct every item:

1. Countries I am legally authorized to work in.
2. Countries or cities where I can physically be present.
3. Remote, hybrid, travel, and timezone tolerance in numbers.
4. Languages I can work in professionally, with one-way limits called out.
5. Minimum years, title level, and role scope I can credibly claim.
6. Hard disqualifiers.
7. Compensation anchors: "look twice", "seriously consider", "drop everything".
8. Current availability and earliest start date.

After the factual pass, return every assumption as a numbered list and make me confirm or correct item by item.

## Hard gates vs trade-offs

Ask:

> Which of these are hard gates where no amount of upside fixes it? Which are trade-offs where a strong role can compensate?

For soft preferences, force weights from 1 to 5:

- Compensation.
- Remote flexibility.
- Domain interest.
- Learning curve.
- Reputation value.
- Energy cost.
- Manager / team quality.
- Side-project compatibility.

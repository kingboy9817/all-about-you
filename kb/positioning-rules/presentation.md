# Presentation

Use this to capture lead-with-strength rules, public-safe ordering, and topics the engine should not raise unprompted.

## Lead with

- Practical systems that made a team faster or clearer.
- Evidence of judgment under ambiguous ownership.
- The user's ability to translate messy needs into usable workflows.

## Do not raise unprompted

- Employment gaps unless the application explicitly asks.
- Compensation history.
- Internal team conflict or politics.

## Ethos rules

- State strengths directly before discussing gaps.
- Prefer "here is the evidence" over self-description.
- Keep caveats factual and brief when they are unavoidable.

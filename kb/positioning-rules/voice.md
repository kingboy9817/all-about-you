# Voice

Populate this with the user's authentic vocabulary and formatting rules. Treat it as a source of truth for drafts, not a style suggestion.

## Authentic vocabulary

- "practical automation" is permitted when the work really removed manual steps.
- "operator-friendly" is permitted for tools designed around daily users.
- "clear owner" is permitted when describing accountability.

## Banned phrases

- Do not use "passionate about innovation" unless the user explicitly says it.
- Do not call routine spreadsheet cleanup "AI transformation".
- Do not describe ordinary coordination as "strategic leadership".

## Formatting preferences

- Prefer short paragraphs with concrete evidence.
- Use bullets for proof points, not for vague traits.
- Keep first-person direct and calm.

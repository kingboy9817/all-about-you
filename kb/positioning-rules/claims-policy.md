# Claims Policy

Use this to define the user's line between framing and fabrication. A claim is usable only when the KB contains evidence or the user confirms it in the same session.

## Permitted framing

- "Built a lightweight routing workflow" is allowed when the artifact existed and was used.
- "Worked with customer operations leaders" is allowed when the user directly supported those stakeholders.
- "Improved reporting cadence" is allowed when before/after timing is documented.

## Not permitted

- Do not imply direct budget ownership without a KB fact.
- Do not convert collaboration into sole ownership.
- Do not invent metrics to make a result sound larger.

## Needs confirmation

- Exact adoption count.
- Current availability.
- Whether a private internal artifact may be described externally.
